We are Revamp

The Coaching Professionals.

CHOOSE US.Because your child is more than a number on a scale.

No other team provides individualized care to get kids in shape while utilizing technology and face-to-face interaction. Our social enterprise empowers, entertains, and educates by having licensed professionals guide your family and child through the Operation Superhero Program.

OUR MISSION.To transform your child into a superhero.

At Revamp, we helps kids achieve weight and lifestyle goals while instilling confidence to help them change the world. Our professionals work diligently to make a healthy lifestyle fit into YOUR schedule so having a healthy household is as simple and practical as can be. Child by child, family by family, we want to make the world a healthier, more beautiful place.

WHAT WE DO.Investing in your child’s future.

Our team of Revamp Coaches, Personal Trainers, Nutritionists, and Therapy Professionals work to motivate your child and family towards success. We combine home site visits, phone calls, unmatched accountability, and a collaborative approach to meet set goals. We work to find the sweet spot between personal interaction and technology to best ensure personal victory.

Our SERVICES

A List of Revamp Offerings.

Initial Consult

· Review The Operation Superhero Program
· Meet Revamp team member at your home
· Assess suitability for program
· Complete Fitness Assessment
· Discuss tailored program to meet individual needs

Nutritional Education Sessions

· Meet with registered nutritionist
· Get answers to nutrition matters
· Discover new ways to eat healthier
· Co-create a prescriptive meal plan
· Learn to navigate the grocery store for healthy options

Therapy Professional Sessions

· Combat personal challenges · Gain knowledge to better communicate your goals to others
· Quit negative habits such as emotional eating and picky eating
· Set realistic goals and deadlines - then meet them!
· Integrate positive habits into routines
· Utilize positive coping skills

Personal Training Sessions

· Learn to get fit anywhere you are; workout with or without equipment
· Establish habits to prevent injury during workouts
· Stay accountable with the help of our team
· Acquire the skills to design your own workouts
· Learn about basic anatomy and self care

Extensive Documentation of Progress

· Before and after photo options
· Regular measurement of: Waist to hip ratio, Body Mass Index (BMI), sleep quality and quantity, food and drink intake, and activity

Revamp Accountability Promise

· Open communication with the Revamp team to stay motivated
· Minimum of bi-weekly check-ins with the family or child
· Professionals remain committed to growth and consistency throughout the program
