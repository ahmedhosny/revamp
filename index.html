We are Revamp

The Coaching Professionals.

CHOOSE US.Because your child is more than a number on a scale.

No other team provides individualized care to get kids in shape while utilizing technology and face-to-face interaction. Our social enterprise empowers, entertains, and educates by having licensed professionals guide your family and child through the Operation Superhero Program.

OUR MISSION.To transform your child into a superhero.

At Revamp, we helps kids achieve weight and lifestyle goals while instilling confidence to help them change the world. Our professionals work diligently to make a healthy lifestyle fit into YOUR schedule so having a healthy household is as simple and practical as can be. Child by child, family by family, we want to make the world a healthier, more beautiful place.

WHAT WE DO.Investing in your child’s future.

Our team of Revamp Coaches, Personal Trainers, Nutritionists, and Therapy Professionals work to motivate your child and family towards success. We combine home site visits, phone calls, unmatched accountability, and a collaborative approach to meet set goals. We work to find the sweet spot between personal interaction and technology to best ensure personal victory.
